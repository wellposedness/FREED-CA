\\begin{lemma}

The intersection of an arbitrary family of compact controlled forward invariant sets is compact and controlled forward invariant.

\\end{lemma}

\\begin{proof}

This is a standard result for statewise controlled invariance; see, for example, \\cite\[Proposition\~2.1\]{kawan2013}. Compactness is immediate as the intersection of compact subsets in a Hausdorff space.

\\end{enumerate}
