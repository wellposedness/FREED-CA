To reason is to exist physically. To think is to burn. To be is to be built.

A Voronoi foam where each cell occupies exactly the territory it can thermodynamically defend. Boundaries aren't walls — they're pressure equilibria between competing cognitive strategies. The hidden physics is the Mandelbrot set, zooming continuously toward aperiodic complexity. Prediction error costs energy. Inaccuracy kills.

Six Cognitive Types

Two axes: loop depth (Builder → Navigator → Drifter) and grounding (Physics vs Symbol). Physics cells predict against the true Mandelbrot band. Symbol cells predict against their own lagged internal map — and pay a divergence tax whenever reality moves faster than the map updates.

Physics Builders (cyan) run the full RSA loop against territory. Symbol Builders (violet) run the same deep loop but against their own representation — fine in stable zones, catastrophic at the Mandelbrot boundary where the map lags reality.

Physics Navigators (green) and Symbol Navigators (amber) use shallow 3-neighbor prediction. Physics Drifters (orange) and Symbol Drifters (rose) are pure noise — Symbol Drifters additionally bleed energy through divergence tax even when their cost is otherwise identical.

The Red Phase

Things get red before they get blue. Learning is thermodynamically expensive before it is thermodynamically efficient. The Wasserstein Floor: the minimum cost path through model-space still burns entropy. No shortcut through the red phase — every mind traverses it.

Variable Population

Dead cells stay dead. Population can now genuinely contract during hard phases and expand when strategies succeed. The sparkline tracks alive count over the last 120 steps. Watch whether Builders expand during high-complexity Mandelbrot epochs and contract during low-complexity ones.

Spontaneous generation: ~1 random cell per 50 steps reanimates with a random type and fresh weights. This represents thermodynamic fluctuations — enough to prevent extinction, not enough to dominate dynamics.

Zipf Prediction

With unconstrained population, true power-law competition is now possible. At γ=1 criticality, strategy frequencies should follow a power law: dominant type at ~2× the second, second at ~1.5× the third. Equal proportions = frozen (γ>1). Complete dominance = dissipative (γ<1). The ZIPF indicator shows whether the current distribution hits the critical ratio.

The Dyadic Soliton prediction: Builders should never go fully extinct even when Navigators dominate — the minimum viable Builder population is load-bearing for the ecosystem.
