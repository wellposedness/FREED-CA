What This Is

A Voronoi foam: each cell occupies exactly the territory closest to its seed point — the minimum energy partition of space. Boundaries aren't walls; they're equilibria between competing pressure gradients. No cell drifts off the edge because there is no edge, only neighboring pressure. This is γ=1 expressed as geometry.

The hidden physics is the Mandelbrot set: zn+1 = z²n + c. Each cell's seed maps to a point c in the complex plane. Its true state is the escape velocity band. Each step the viewport zooms 0.5% toward c = −0.75 + 0.1i (cardioid/bulb boundary — aperiodic at every scale). Cells see their Voronoi neighbors' bands and predict their own next band. Prediction error costs energy. Inaccuracy kills.

Cell color encodes position in the error × complexity tradeoff space. Cyan: low error, low weight norm — compression attractor, optimal. Deep red: large model, wrong predictions — maximum entropy production. The foam topology means reproduction spreads into adjacent pressure-zones, not grid positions — a cell colonizes its thermodynamic neighbors.
