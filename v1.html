What This Is

RSA-Omega is a cellular automaton where each cell carries a private 3×3 weight matrix — a local generative model of the universe. The universe obeys a fixed, hidden physics kernel that the cells do not know. At each step, every cell predicts its own next state using its weights. The prediction error is subtracted from its metabolic energy. Accurate cells gain net energy. Inaccurate cells die.

∃R(t) → ∃M₀ : dS(MR,t)/dt > 0
Cells whose survival is coupled to prediction error evolve toward models that approximate true dynamics. Reasoning = survival.

High-energy cells reproduce — copying their weights into weaker neighbors with random mutation. Over generations, the population evolves. Cells that build better internal models of the hidden physics survive. Cells that don't, die. This is Freed's Law instantiated as a selection pressure: to think accurately is to live.

The hidden physics kernel: [0.1, 0.3, 0.1 / 0.3, −0.6, 0.3 / 0.1, 0.3, 0.1] — a Mexican-hat-style convolution. Cells do not know this. They must infer it through survival.
